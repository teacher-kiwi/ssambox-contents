## The Scale of Space — Pixel Space

An interactive simulation that lets you experience the true scale of the solar system when the Moon's diameter is shrunk to just 1 pixel.

### How to Use

- **Mouse scroll / drag**: Move left and right
- **Arrow keys (←→)**: Move left and right
- **Spacebar**: Start/stop auto-scroll at light speed
- **Speed buttons (bottom)**: Stop / Light(1×) / 100× / 1000× / 10000× / Warp
- **Minimap (top)**: Click to jump to any location
- **Fullscreen button**: Toggle fullscreen

### Things to Notice

🌕 If the Moon is only 1 pixel, how big is the Sun?
🌍 Feel the distance from Earth to Mars by scrolling.
🪐 Experience the vast empty space between Jupiter and Saturn.
🚀 How long would it take to reach Pluto even at the speed of light?

### Learning Goals

- Understand the actual size ratios of solar system bodies.
- Experience the immense distances in the solar system firsthand.
- Compare the speed of light with cosmic distances to grasp the scale of space.
