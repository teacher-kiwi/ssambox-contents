## Solar System Simulation

This is an interactive simulation where you can observe the sun and its 8 planets revolving in real-time.

### How to use

- **Mouse Drag**: Move around the solar system
- **Scroll**: Zoom In / Zoom Out
- **Hover over a planet**: Display planet information
- **F Key** or **Fullscreen Button**: Toggle Fullscreen
